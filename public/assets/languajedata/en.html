    Our game

    Unique system of obtaining cards

    Where you can buy a single pack to have all the cards corresponding to that pack, because learning does not come from repeated purchase of the same book, but from repeated reading of the same and other books.

    Games based on debates

    In order to show the modus operandi, and daily life of a philosopher, we try to make the form of duels close to a contest in which the fray is given by means of eristics, or the fight with words. Our cards are therefore representations of philosophical concepts (to a greater or lesser extent)

    Ludification of philosophical knowledge

    With our game we approach, in a mechanical way, some relations between philosophical concepts of real life, all in order to understand, in an entertaining way, how the thought of the different philosophers is structured. As a good form of gamification and gamification, you learn by playing, maybe it won't help you to get your PhD, but at least you'll be able to have a bar conversation with the drunkard on duty and not feel overwhelmed by what he says.

    Our cards

    This is the time, or amount of resource to pay for using the card or concept.

    This is the name of the card, it is called like each philosophical concept, it is normal that several books have concepts with the same name, but they work in a different way, just as the idea in Descartes is not the same as the idea in Plato.

    This is the number of points that you make your opponent lose, each argument that is launched in a debate can serve to strengthen and weaken a position, that is what eristics, or fighting with words, is all about.

    This statistic measures the amount of conviction points you are going to get, when you reach more than 10 points you win, negative points are nothing to lose, they just take you further away from the 10 point target.

    This is the type of card and there are 3 concepts that you can drop on your turn, paying their cost. Replicas are cards that serve as a response to the concepts, so that the player who played the concept can jump in with another concept, simulating a change of theme, or a counter-reply. Generally speaking, the counter-replica is a more powerful card than the concept.

    There are currently only 3 effects associated with the card, the first one is search that gives you cards according to the description, draw that makes you draw as many cards as it appears in the description, reference of another card in which if it is not there, you can't play it even if you have the cost or time to play it.
